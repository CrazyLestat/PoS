Make use of the right technologies

Our platform uses the ERC-1155 - The MultiToken Standard which is designed to combine non-fungible (ERC-721) and fungible (ERC-20) tokens in a single smart contract. Using ERC-1155 increases your security and decreases the redundant bytecode on the Ethereum blockchain.

The Wolfs NFTs will be stored as an ERC-1155 token on Ethereum blockchain and it is hosted on IPFS.
